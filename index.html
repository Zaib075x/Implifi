What is Implifi?

Implifi is a virtual learning platform that boosts your practical learning experience in technology by moving your traditional laboratories in universities and making them more immersive and unique. With Implifi, you can refer to lab manual while also practicing and execute the lab programs at one place without any dependencies.


Why Implifi?

Implifi offers the services of computer science labs in your educational institution at anywhere, anytime. With us, the students can perform lab exercises easily while getting evaluated by their teachers in the same platform.

What do we aim?

To increase interest and eagerness among students to acquire knowledge through unique ways.

To make students learn new things according to their own pace and convenience.

To provide variety of experiments to students for practice and improving their skills.

For making practical sessions indubious by coming up many tools and resources for teachers to easily explain and guide while evaluating their performance.
